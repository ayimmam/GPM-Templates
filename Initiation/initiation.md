# Project Initiation Templates

This folder contains templates used during the **project initiation phase**, where project goals, scope, and stakeholders are formally defined.

These templates are foundational for:
- Project discovery
- Stakeholder alignment
- Business justification
- Early documentation in project management certificates

---

## Templates Included

### Project Charter
Defines the high-level structure of a project, including:
- Business problem or opportunity
- Project goals and success criteria
- Scope (in scope / out of scope)
- Constraints and assumptions
- Key stakeholders

Link:
- [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jAkGJ0z39tj9XPW3J9Bp6kxQ2fdB07QJReRo2UiLy0E/template/preview)

### Stakeholder Analysis Matrix
Used to:
- Identify internal and external stakeholders
- Assess influence and interest
- Plan stakeholder engagement strategies

## When to Use These Templates
Use these documents at the start of a project, before detailed planning begins.  
They are commonly required artifacts in **entry-level project manager portfolios**.
