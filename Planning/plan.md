# Project Planning Templates

This folder contains templates used to **translate project goals into an executable plan**.

These templates are essential for:
- Scheduling
- Risk management
- Role clarity
- Communication planning

---

## Templates Included

### Project Plan
Documents:
- Milestones and deliverables
- Dependencies
- Timeline assumptions

Link:
- [Google Sheets](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1n3DdsjSXg-YOO_15ImEMUTNItmp2CZ5pFDlUOx1mx5E/template/preview)

---

### RACI Matrix
Clarifies responsibilities by identifying:
- Responsible
- Accountable
- Consulted
- Informed

Link:
- [Google Sheets](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1vXT3juD4LSJ8YgJZZ3GCES8otG2hI9_lwIh6doVTLyg/template/preview)

### Risk Register
Tracks:
- Project risks
- Probability and impact
- Mitigation strategies
- Risk owners

Link:
- [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1TaTeMogSAzkSsXBB64E4eKbGiKVdc6v8l56vVi8tNSA/template/preview)

### Communication Plan

- What information is shared
- With whom

- How often

## When to Use These Templates
These templates are used after project approval and before execution begins.  
They are frequently referenced in **project management interviews and assessments**.
