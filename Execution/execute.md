# Project Execution Templates

This folder includes templates used during **active project execution**, when work is being delivered and coordinated.

They support:
- Team alignment
- Stakeholder communication
- Change management

---

## Templates Included

### Project Status Report
Used for:
- Weekly or bi-weekly updates
- Sponsor and leadership communication
- Tracking progress and blockers

Link:
- [Google Sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1oFF_rbKVjvLaUgHB-a_6W0FghqQ8nScKbrPsrkFTtjc/template/preview)

---

### Change Request Log
Captures:
- Scope, timeline, or cost changes
- Impact analysis
- Approval decisions

Link:
- [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Txsx5U13eEKBV_qV9n2LKU7mYPqrF4OZPpazNpf6HbM/template/preview)

---

## When to Use These Templates
Use these documents once execution begins and throughout delivery.  
They demonstrate **operational discipline and accountability**.
