## Template Overview

### Project Initiation Templates

#### Project Charter
Defines the foundation of a project, including:
- Business problem or opportunity
- Goals and success criteria
- High-level scope (in scope / out of scope)
- Stakeholders and constraints

Commonly used in **project management certificate programs** and early discovery phases.

Format: Google Docs

---

#### Stakeholder Analysis Matrix
Used to:
- Identify key stakeholders
- Map influence and interest
- Define engagement strategies

Essential for managing expectations in cross-functional teams.

Format: Google Sheets

---

### Project Planning Templates

#### Project Plan
Includes:
- Milestones and deliverables
- Dependencies and assumptions
- Timeline structure

Frequently requested in **PM interviews and portfolios**.

Format: Google Docs or Google Sheets

---

#### RACI Matrix
Clarifies accountability by defining:
- Responsible
- Accountable
- Consulted
- Informed

Highly applicable in Agile, hybrid, and operational projects.

Format: Google Sheets

---

#### Risk Register
Tracks:
- Identified risks
- Probability and impact
- Mitigation strategies
- Risk ownership

A core artifact for **risk management in project management**.

Format: Google Sheets

---

#### Communication Plan
Documents:
- What information is shared
- With whom
- Through which channel
- At what frequency

Improves transparency and reduces stakeholder friction.

Format: Google Docs

---

### Execution and Monitoring Templates

#### Project Status Report
Used for:
- Weekly or bi-weekly updates
- Sponsor communication
- Leadership reporting

Includes progress summaries, blockers, and next steps.

Format: Google Docs

---

#### Change Request Log
Captures:
- Scope changes
- Schedule and cost impact
- Approval decisions

Critical for scope control and auditability.

Format: Google Sheets

---

### Project Closure Templates

#### Project Closeout Report (Primary Case Example)

Sample reference: **Sauce & Spoon Digital Tabletop Menu Project** :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

This closeout report template includes:
- Project summary and objectives
- Methodology (Agile / hybrid)
- Planned vs actual schedule, cost, and scope

#### Lessons Learned Register
Supports:
- Continuous improvement
- Organizational learning
- Future project optimization

Often requested in **NGOs, enterprise projects, and academic research projects**.

Format: Google Docs or Google Sheets
